Blocky Pasta

Description

Bolognese or bolognese ( Italian : ragù bolognese , pronounced /ragu boloɲɲeze/ ) is a sauce very commonly used to accompany pasta (one of the most common is tagliatelle al ragu ) or polenta

t is a thick, brown-red sauce, widely used in the regions near Bologna . In Italian it bears the name ragù as a deformation of the French ragoût .

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • 454 g ground beef>
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • ½ cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 sachet of MAGGI Bolognese Sauce Base
  • 3 cups of water
  • 750 g of cooked noodle-type pasta
  • parmesan cheese to taste

Preparation

  1. In a pot over medium heat, pour the oil and sauté the garlic and onion for 2 minutes or until golden.
  2. Add the meat and cook for 5 min, stirring constantly.
  3. In a bowl, dissolve the MAGGI Bolognese Sauce Base sachet in the water, pour into the pot and stir. Add the wine, combine and cook over low heat for 15 min.
  4. Pour the pasta and stir well to integrate all the ingredients. It serves.
  5. Enjoy this delicious creamy bolognese pasta with MAGGI® Bolognese Sauce Base.
